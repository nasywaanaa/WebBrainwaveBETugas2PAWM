Frequently Asked Questions

What is Brainwave?

Brainwave is an online learning platform designed to help students enhance their skills and knowledge through various educational modules.

How do I sign up?

To sign up, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill out the required details, and create your account.

What courses are available?

We offer a variety of courses, including topics in science, technology, arts, and more. You can explore all available courses under the 'Courses' section.

How do I track my learning progress?

You can track your learning progress by visiting the 'Tracker' section, which shows detailed reports on your course progress, completed modules, and upcoming lessons.

Who can I contact for support?

For any questions or support, please visit our 'Support' page or reach out via the 'Contact Us' form available on the website.
